Famous blogger/vlogger in the Philippines

Cong TV
@CongTheVlogger

Lincoln Cortez Velasquez, better known by his username Cong TV, is a Filipino YouTuber, comedian, musician, vlogger, and online streamer. He is known for his comedy videos on YouTube. Velasquez is often also considered one of the top YouTube personalities in the Philippines.

Velasquez began uploading videos to YouTube in 2008. He started out as a vlogger and rose to fame in the Philippines by uploading comedy skits and parodies to the platform. He also played the role of Moby in the 2016 Philippine television series My Super D. Aside from being a vlogger, he is also a live streamer and a musician. He is the lead vocalist and guitarist of the band Coln, which he formed in 2018. He released his first music video as well in the same year on YouTube.On December 3, 2020, his channel was included and ranked third on the list of the Top YouTube PH Creators of 2020.

Wil Dasovich

@WilDasovichVlogs

Wil Dasovich is a Filipino-American vlogger who rose to fame through his vlogs about his life and adventures in the Philippines. He started his vlog in 2016, documenting his experiences while traveling and living in the country.

Since then, Wil Dasovich has become one of the most popular vloggers in the Philippines, with a massive following on social media. He has also collaborated with other popular vloggers and influencers in the country, as well as with international brands. Although Wil Dasovich's vlogging career has had its ups and downs, he continues to be a prominent figure in the Filipino vlogging community. His content is varied, covering topics such as travel, food, and lifestyle, and he is known for his infectious energy and enthusiasm. If you're interested in watching Wil Dasovich's vlogs, you can find them on his YouTube channel or on his social media accounts.
